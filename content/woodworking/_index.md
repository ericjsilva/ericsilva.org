Welcome to my woodworking project documentation. Each guide follows the same structure so you know what to expect and can jump to the details you need:

- Project Overview: what I built and why
- Inspiration: the spark behind the design choice
- Wood Type and Fasteners: species, dimensions, and hardware
- Tools Used: the essentials I relied on
- Finishing: stain/paint/oil, application, and protection notes
- Photos: in-progress and finished results

Whether you're tackling your first build or looking for your next weekend project, I hope these write-ups help you create something both functional and beautiful.
