Welcome to my laser engraving project documentation. These guides detail the materials used, settings applied, and the digital files required to recreate the piece:

- **Project Overview**: What the project is and why it was made.
- **Material**: Information on the primary materials (wood, slate, acrylic, etc.) and dimensions.
- **Equipment & Settings**: Laser types, speed, and power settings that worked for the material.
- **Design Files**: Links to the Lightburn file or source images and vectors used.
- **The Engraving**: High-quality images of the finished piece.
- **Process & Notes**: The process to achieve the finish, including any post-engraving treatments.

I hope these examples and templates provide a useful starting point for your own engraving projects!
